Welcome!

I am a Senior Research Scientist at the Allen Institute for AI. I work on many things, including building large language models like OLMo, evaluating large language models, creating and documenting the contents of web-scale training datasets, the environmental impact of AI, and improving transparency and reproducibility in the research community.
You can find a list of my publications here (though my Google Scholar may be more up-to-date).
You can find a bio for invited talks here.
I also spend a lot of time talking to the press and giving talks; please email me directly at jessed@allenai.org for press inquiries and invitations to speak.
I receive a lot of email and it would take all my time if I let it, so unfortunately I don't make it through all my email. If it's something someone else can help with it's often a good idea to CC them.
