Press and Media Coverage

My work is regularly covered by the press including the New York Times (4x), the Wall Street Journal, WIRED (4x), Forbes, and MIT Tech Review (2x). You can find a list of press articles covering my work or quoting me at the bottom of this page.

In addition, I am regularly interviewed about AI on topics of public interest, especially the climate impact of AI (BCC World Service's Newshour, NPR's Science Friday, KTVU Fox, etc.), how training datasets are created for large AI models (New York Times Magazine, Washington Post, etc.), and AI ethics (TechCrunch, The New Yorker, etc.).

Please reach out to me at jessed@allenai.org for press inquiries.
